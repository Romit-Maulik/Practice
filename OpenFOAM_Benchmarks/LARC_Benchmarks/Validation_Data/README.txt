These are the data files for reporting your results for the Collaborative Challenge.
There are 5 cases.  The 2DN00 case includes 4 different runs (4 angles of attack).

Submission instructions:
 1. After filling the files out with your results, put all the files in a directory.
 2. If you do not have particular results, then delete the corresponding files.
 2. Either zip or tar+gzip the directory, and email to: C.L.Rumsey@nasa.gov by the deadline.

2DWMH:
   2DWMH_u_at7stations.dat  - for reporting u profiles along y direction at 7 x-stations
   2DWMH_uv_at7stations.dat - for reporting u'v' profiles along y direction at 7 x-stations
